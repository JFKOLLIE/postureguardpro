How long should I wear PostureGuard Pro each day?

Is it comfortable to wear?

Yes! PostureGuard Pro weighs only 60 grams and is made from soft, food-grade silicone. Most users forget they're wearing it after the first few days.

Will it work for severe posture problems?

PostureGuard Pro helps with mild to moderate posture issues. For severe spinal conditions, we recommend consulting your healthcare provider first.
